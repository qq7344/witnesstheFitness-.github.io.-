Witness the Fitness

"Promote social interaction in a casual gym environment"

"A mobile application that allows users to connect and quickly contact other members of their gym"

Leader-board

Encourages gym-members to partner up and compete for challenges and prizes. It is our goal that by making the initial connection between new people as simple as possible, more of our target audience will work collaboratively in the gym. Also, by gamifying the gym experience and adding a competitive element, we are certain people will in-turn be more social and outgoing in the gym.

Chat

Allow user to chat other members in the gym. Even you are far away from the gym and you can still ask somebody to workout together in anytime.

Matching System

Allow user to find a workout buddy and workout together.User can find aother users to workout together to earn points in the Leader-board. The system shows all the training purpose of every user so other users can find their buddy by their preference.

User Profile

User can set their information here such as gender, name, email address. Moreover user can set some information to help they get in match including workout time and interests.

The inspiration

We found out there is lack of commucation in the gym so we designed this idea to help promote social interaction in a casual gym environment. Specifically, our product is designed to help young adults who go to the gym on a semi-regular basis meet new people to train with. This idea was inspired by our initial research, which identified that both new and experienced gym-goers want to meet new people at the gym but often lack the confidence to do so.
